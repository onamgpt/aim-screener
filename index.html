How it works

No predictions, no tips, no gurus. AIM is pure arithmetic: when a stock drifts far enough from its control value, the formula computes a rebalancing quantity. You study the output and make your own decision.

STEP 1

Enter your portfolio

Add your holdings — quantity and average price — for Indian and US stocks. Set your cash pool, SAFE band and trigger. Your data syncs securely to your account.

STEP 2

Run the AIM analysis

Live prices are fetched and the Lichello formula runs on every holding: portfolio value vs control value, SAFE zone, trigger filter, profit-check on the sell side.

STEP 3

Study, learn, decide

See exactly what the model computes and why — every output comes with its reasoning. Backtest AIM against buy-and-hold on any stock before you trust it.
